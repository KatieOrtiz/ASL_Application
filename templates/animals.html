ASL Signs for Animals

 

Discover a collection of American Sign Language (ASL) signs for various animals. Learning signs for animals can be both fun and educational. Below are a few common animal signs:

Dog

Fingerspell D-O-G and move your hand down as if you're petting a dog's head.

 

Cat

Make the ASL letter "C" by forming a "C" shape with your hand and stroke your cheek with your index finger.

 

Bird

Hold your hand like you're grasping a bird and flap your thumb and fingers to mimic wings.

 
 

Exploring ASL signs for animals can enhance your vocabulary and communication skills in sign language. Feel free to practice and learn more signs for various animals!

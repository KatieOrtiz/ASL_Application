ASL Food Signs

 

Apple

Hold a fist representing the apple, then twist the wrist as if turning a doorknob.

Banana

Hold an imaginary banana in one hand, then peel it with the other hand.

Pizza

Flat-hand gesture mimicking the shape of a pizza while tapping fingers as if sprinkling toppings.

Frequently Asked Questions about ASL

 

What is American Sign Language (ASL)?

ASL is a complete, complex language that employs signs made with the hands, facial expressions, and body postures to communicate.

Is ASL the same worldwide?

No, sign languages vary across countries and regions. ASL used in the United States, for example, differs from British Sign Language (BSL).

Can anyone learn ASL?

Absolutely! ASL is open for everyone to learn and can be beneficial for communication with Deaf individuals or those in the Deaf community.
